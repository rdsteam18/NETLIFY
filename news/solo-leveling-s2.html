New Season of Demon Slayer Announced for Fall 2023

June 15, 2023 By AnimeNews Network 24,589 Views
Demon Slayer New Season Ufotable Fall 2023

Ufotable has officially confirmed the production of a new season of the wildly popular anime series "Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba," focusing on the Hashira Training arc. The announcement was made during a special livestream event celebrating the series' fifth anniversary.

What to Expect in the New Season

The upcoming season will adapt the Hashira Training arc from Koyoharu Gotouge's original manga, which follows Tanjiro Kamado's intensive training with the elite Hashira warriors to prepare for the final battle against Muzan Kibutsuji and his demon forces.

Series producer Akifumi Fujio revealed that this season will feature some of the most visually stunning animation sequences yet, with the studio pushing their signature effects to new heights. "We want to surpass what we achieved with the Entertainment District arc," Fujio stated during the announcement.

Release Details

The new season is scheduled to premiere in October 2023 as part of the fall anime season. While the exact date hasn't been confirmed, industry insiders suggest it will likely debut in the first week of October, following the pattern of previous seasons.

International streaming will be available through Crunchyroll and Funimation, with simuldubs expected to follow shortly after the Japanese broadcast. The season is projected to run for approximately 12 episodes, covering the complete Hashira Training arc.

Cast and Staff Returns

The main cast members, including Natsuki Hanae as Tanjiro, Akari Kitō as Nezuko, and Yoshitsugu Matsuoka as Inosuke, are all confirmed to return for the new season. The production will maintain the same core staff, with Haruo Sotozaki directing and Akira Matsushima handling character designs.

Composer Yuki Kajiura and Go Shiina will once again collaborate on the soundtrack, with the opening theme performed by LiSA, continuing her streak as the series' musical voice since the first season.

Fans can expect more details, including the exact premiere date and additional cast announcements, to be revealed during Anime Expo in July, where the Demon Slayer team will hold a special panel.
