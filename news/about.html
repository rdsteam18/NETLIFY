We Live and Breathe Anime

NETLIFY is more than just a streaming platform - we're a passionate community dedicated to bringing the best of Japanese animation to fans worldwide.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by RDS TEAM, NETLIFY began as a small project to share our love for Japanese animation with like-minded fans. What started as a hobby quickly grew into one of the most respected anime streaming platforms, serving millions of fans across the globe.

Our mission has always been simple: to provide high-quality, legal anime streaming with the best user experience possible. We work directly with Japanese studios and licensors to bring you the latest shows as soon as they're available, often with multiple subtitle languages.

Today, NETLIFY boasts one of the largest anime libraries outside Japan, with over 10,000 episodes from more than 500 series. But we're not just about quantity - our curated collections, expert recommendations, and community features help fans discover new favorites and connect with each other.
