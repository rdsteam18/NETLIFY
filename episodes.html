My Hero Academia

Synopsis

Izuku has dreamt of being a hero all his life—a lofty goal for anyone, but especially challenging for a kid with no superpowers! That’s right, in a world where eighty percent of the population has some kind of super-powered quirk, Izuku was unlucky enough to be born completely normal. But that’s not going to stop him from enrolling in one of the world’s most prestigious hero academies.

My Hero Academia is an anime that follows the story of Izuku, a boy who dreams of becoming a hero in a world where almost everyone has superpowers. Despite being born without any special abilities, Izuku's determination leads him to enroll in a prestigious hero academy. The series is a captivating blend of action, humor, and heartfelt moments that explore the true meaning of heroism.
