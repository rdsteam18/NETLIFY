Ramayana: The Legend of Prince Rama

About the Movie

'Ramayana: The Legend of Prince Rama' is a beautifully animated adaptation of the ancient Indian epic, Ramayana. Released in 1993, this film brings to life the heroic journey of Lord Rama, his love for Sita, and his battle against the demon king Ravana. The movie is known for its stunning animation, powerful storytelling, and deep spiritual significance.

Experience the divine tale of duty, honor, and devotion through this timeless classic. Whether you are a fan of mythology or simply love great animation, this film is a must-watch.
